About the Event

Join us for an evening of giving and celebration at our Annual Charity Gala. All proceeds go towards our clean water initiative across three continents. This year, we are focusing on sustainable infrastructure development in rural communities.

Expect a night of inspiring stories, networking with community leaders, and a silent auction featuring works from local artists. Dress code is formal attire.
